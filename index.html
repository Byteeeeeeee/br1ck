BR1CK

By Copernicium (Click me!)

How It Works

The exploit works because of an issue CR50s have when enrolling. If you reset it at just the right time, it will corrupt data in the FWMP TPM space, resulting in a bricked FWMP. This allows us to boot into shimmer with CTRL + U and clear FWMP for when we fix the brick.

How I Found It

While trying to do CRSH2TTY, I ended up bricking my device multiple times (a total of four). One time, I randomly tried to disable write protection while unbricking with a SuzyQ, and surprisingly, it worked!
All of my exploits have been made due to CRSH2TTY in some way.

How to Do It

1. Go to chrome://network#logs
2. Upload the file to the form below and remember the time it gives you.
3. Powerwash and go through the regular setup until the "Getting device ready" screen.
4. Get a stopwatch ready. When you're at the correct time, perform an EC-Reset by holding Esc + Refresh and then clicking Power.
5. Reboot (Refresh + Power). If you're still in recovery mode, click TAB. If the recovery reason is not "Recovery Button Pressed", continue! Otherwise, retry until you get it.
6. Boot a shim with CTRL + U and run deprovision.
7. In shimmer, open a bash terminal and run gsctool -a -o.
8. At the end of this process, you should reboot unenrolled and unbricked.
